# Demo Flow

## 1. Problem

Start with the market problem: privacy-first institutional networks protect sensitive positions, but they also fragment shared market intelligence and make benchmarking difficult.

## 2. Network Intelligence Overview

Show the high-level console and explain that Compass converts privacy-preserving contribution into shared institutional intelligence without exposing raw positions.

## 3. Contribution Campaign

Walk through the active campaign, the requested fields, the contribution policy, and why contributor quality and attestation level matter.

## 4. Confidential Processing

Explain the confidential compute boundary: raw data enters the secure environment, deterministic analytics run inside it, persistent raw storage is disabled, and only derived intelligence exits.

## 5. Benchmark Intelligence

Show anonymized, trust-weighted benchmark outputs for repo and treasury scenarios. Highlight reliability, dispersion, and stress signals.

## 6. My Position Intelligence

Display the institution-scoped comparison with benchmark deltas, confidence, collateral structure context, and the local explainable summary.

## 7. Record to Canton

Close by showing that the result can be recorded as an auditable output on Canton, reinforcing the workflow and governance angle.
